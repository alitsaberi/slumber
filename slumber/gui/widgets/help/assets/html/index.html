Frequently Asked Questions

  • Solid Blue: The device is on.
  • Blinking Blue: The device is off.
  • Solid White: The device is charging.
  • No Light: The device has no battery.

If the EEG headband does not react to the power button, it may need a reset:

  1. Locate the small reset button on the back of the device.
  2. Use a sharp object (e.g., a paperclip) to press and hold the reset button.
  3. You should hear a sound and see the blue light turn on again.

If you continue to experience issues, please contact the study coordinator.
