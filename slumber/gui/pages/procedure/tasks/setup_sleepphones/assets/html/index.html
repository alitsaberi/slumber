How to Put on Your Headphones

  1. 1. Locate the flat speakers inside the headband. One is on each side.

  2. 2. Place the headband on your head, ensuring the tag is at the back.

  3. 3. Adjust the speakers so they are positioned directly over your ears.

How to Turn On Your Headphones

  1. 1. Remove the Bluetooth® module from the hook-and-loop opening at the back of the headband.

  2. 2. Press and hold the center button on the module for 5 seconds until you hear ascending tones and see a blinking red light.

How to Charge Your Headphones

  1. 1. Open the hook-and-loop opening and remove the Bluetooth® module.

  2. 2. Plug the micro-USB cable into the module (ensure the USB symbol faces you).

  3. 3. Connect the cable to a USB port or wall adapter. A red light will appear during charging.
